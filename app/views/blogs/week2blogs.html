I researched the difference between margin border and padding because I was a little unclear. It turns out to be rather simple. Margin is just that, a margin around the idem you are working with. It is transparent and the out most layer. It prevents overlapping of other items. Border is visible, based on your settings. You can choose color and type of line. It sits inside the margin and wraps the padding/content. Which brings us to padding. Padding is the clear space, or colored space, in-between the content and the border. This keeps the content from touching the border. And the last layer is content. Which is the content, no explanation needed there?
